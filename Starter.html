Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of red Lentils
  • 1 Medium size Onion
  • 1 Tablespoon of Flour
  • 2 Tablespoons of Oil
  • 6 Cups of hot Water ~1200ml
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon of Turmeric

Recipe

  1. Heat oil in a pot, sauté chopped onions until slightly pink.
  2. Add flour and continue cooking on low heat.
  3. Rinse the lentils.
  4. Pour in water, mix, and cover. Bring to a boil, then simmer until lentils are soft.
  5. Blend the soup until smooth, or strain if you don't have a blender.
  6. Season with black pepper, salt, and optional cumin. Cook for 5 more minutes and remove from heat.
  7. If it's too thick, add some water and simmer briefly.
  8. In a small pan, melt two tablespoons of butter, add a teaspoon of red pepper powder, and remove from heat.
  9. Serve the lentil soup in bowls, drizzle with the red pepper sauce, and garnish with a lemon slice.

Garlic prawns, Camarão ao Alhinho

Ingredients

  • 450g of Prawns
  • 6 Gloves of Garlic
  • 60ml of Olive Oil
  • 60ml of White Wine
  • 30g of salted Butter
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for garnish

Recipe

  1. Heat oil in a frying pan, sauté the Prawns.
  2. Add the Garlic either chopped or mashed
  3. Add Pepper and Salt.
  4. When the Prawns are almost cooked add Butter and White wine
  5. Reduce and sauce until prefered thickness
