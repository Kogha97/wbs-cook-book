Köfte

Ingredients

  • 500g ground meat
  • 1 medium Onion
  • 1 clove of Garlic
  • 1 Egg
  • Half a tea glass of breadcrumbs
  • 1 tablespoon of Oil
  • 1 handful of finely chopped Parsley
  • 1.5 teaspoons of Salt
  • Half a teaspoon of Black Pepper
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin

Recipe

  1. Combine minced meat, grated onion, chopped garlic, egg, bread crumbs, oil, parsley, salt, black pepper, and cumin in a mixing bowl. Knead until well mixed.
  2. Cover the mixture with plastic wrap and refrigerate for about 30 minutes./li>
  3. Add Pepper and Salt.
  4. After refrigerating, shape the mixture into meatballs by tearing off pieces with your hands. Oval shape works, but any shape is fine as long as they are thin.
  5. lace the shaped meatballs on a plate. You can let them rest at this stage if you like.
  6. Heat oil in a pan on the stove.
  7. Cook the meatballs by turning them on both sides in the heated pan.

Manti

Ingredients

  • 3 water glass Fame
  • 1 water glass warm water
  • 1 piece egg
  • 1 tea spoon salt
  • 250 -gram mince
  • 1 tea spoon salt
  • 1 pieces medium size onion
  • 1.5 teaspoons of salt
  • 1/2 tea spoon black pepper
  • 1/2 tea spoon chili pepper

Recipe

  1. In a deep mixing bowl, combine 3.5 cups of flour and 1 teaspoon of salt.
  2. Create a well in the center of the flour mixture.
  3. Add 1 egg into the well and mix.
  4. Gradually pour in 1 glass of warm water while continuing to mix.
  5. Knead the dough until it reaches a medium consistency, not too hard or soft.
  6. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and let it rest.
  7. While the dough rests, mix 250 grams of lean minced meat, 1 medium-sized onion, 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper, 1 teaspoon of salt, and 1/2 teaspoon of chili pepper in a bowl.
  8. Place the dough on a floured surface and divide it into 3 equal portions.
  9. Roll out each portion into thicker sheets with a rolling pin.
  10. Cut the rolled dough into small squares and place a portion of the minced meat mixture in the center of each square.

Roasted Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 (2.25 to 2.7kg) roasting chicken
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 large bunch fresh thyme, plus 20 sprigs
  • 1 lemon, halved
  • 1 head garlic, cut in half crosswise
  • 30g butter, melted
  • 1 large yellow onion, thickly sliced
  • 4 carrots cut into 5cm chunks
  • 1 bulb of fennel, tops removed, and cut into wedges

Recipe

  1. Preheat the oven to 210°C (Gas mark 7).
  2. Clean the chicken by removing the giblets and rinsing it inside and out. Remove excess fat and any remaining pin feathers. Pat the chicken dry.
  3. Add Pepper and Salt.
  4. Stuff the chicken cavity with thyme, lemon halves, and garlic.
  5. Brush the outside of the chicken with butter and season it with salt and pepper.
  6. Roast the chicken for 1 1/2 hours or until the juices run clear when you cut between a leg and thigh.
  7. Frie Potatos and serve

Duck Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 whole duck
  • Coriander
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 thyme sprig
  • 1 star anise
  • 1 leek (green top only, keep the white part for the rice)
  • 1 onion, halved
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 large orange, zested and juiced
  • For the rice:
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 garlic clove

Recipe

  1. Place the duck in your largest pan.
  2. Cover the duck with water.
  3. Add coriander, bay leaves, thyme, star anise, leek top, onion, carrot, orange zest, and a grinding of black pepper to the pan.
  4. Put the pan over medium heat and bring it to a boil. Be sure to skim off any scum from the surface.
  5. Reduce the heat slightly, cover the pan, and simmer for 45 minutes.
  6. Turn off the heat and let the duck cool in the stock for 15 minutes.
  7. Drain the stock from the duck, but reserve it for cooking the rice.
  8. Allow the duck to rest until it's cool enough to handle, then shred the meat away from the bone.
